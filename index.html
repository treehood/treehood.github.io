FAQ's

1. What time does it actually start?

Our ceremony is scheduled to start at 2:30pm sharp. We want to encourage guests to arrive no later than 2:15pm, please be mindful of traffic and other unforeseen delays. There is lots of free parking on site, and you're welcome to leave your vehicle overnight if you choose.

2. What should I wear?

Our dress code is formal, and we also kindly ask that all guests avoid wearing the bridal party colour; the bridal party colour is burgundy.

3. Can I leave my car?

Yes! You are more than welcome to park your car overnight free of charge.

4. Do you have a hotel block reserved?

We do not have a hotel block reserved, but we have a 10% base rate discount with C Hotel by Carmen's which is located approximately 10 minutes from the venue. To use the discount, just mention our names and wedding date. The address is: 1530 Stone Church Road E, Hamilton, ON, L8W 3P9.

5. Will there be an open bar?

Yes! There will be an open bar, please plan for a safe ride home if you choose to have a drink.

6. Will I be hungry?

We hope not! Right after the ceremony, horderves will be served during cocktail hour, our seated dinner will follow, and we will have a late night food service as well. All allergies that we have been made aware of will be accomodated.
